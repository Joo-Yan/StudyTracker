# Impeccable Design Context

## Brand Identity
**Name:** StudyTracker
**Vibe:** Refined Minimalism. Warm, calm, and focused.
**Palette:** Warm Stone (Stone-50 to Stone-950). No clinical grays.
**Typography:** Manrope (Humanist Sans). Elegant and legible.

## Design Principles
1.  **Warmth over sterility:** Use warm neutrals instead of cool grays.
2.  **Depth over borders:** Use soft shadows and layering to define space, not harsh lines.
3.  **Typography as interface:** rely on weight and scale, not just boxes.
4.  **Generous spacing:** Let the content breathe.

## Technical Details
-   **Font:** `Manrope` (Next.js Google Fonts)
-   **Radius:** `0.75rem` (12px) standard.
-   **Primary Color:** `hsl(24 10% 10%)` (Warm Black)
-   **Background:** `hsl(60 5% 98%)` (Warm Off-White)
